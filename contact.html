What CONTACT does?

    The Contact component is designed to provide users with a way to get in touch with the organization. It includes a contact form and contact information, displayed within a structured layout. The component is responsive, ensuring proper display across different screen sizes.

    Key Features:

  • SectionWrapper: The component is wrapped within a SectionWrapper which provides a consistent layout and styling.
  • Contact Form: A form is provided for users to fill in their contact details, including: Name, Email, Phone Number, Information on how they found the organization, A submit button to send the information.
  • Contact Information: Displays multiple contact phone numbers and an email address using icons and styled text for clear visibility.
  • Google Map: Embeds a Google Map iframe showing the organization's location.
  • Responsive Design: Uses flexbox and responsive classes to ensure the layout adapts to different screen sizes, displaying in a column for small screens and a row for larger screens.
